Cafe Arcana: Tarot

A small indie tarot app project.

Cafe Arcana: Tarot is an indie tarot app created to keep the quiet atmosphere of drawing cards while translating a personal reading experience into a digital format.

The project focuses on a more reflective tarot experience: asking your own question, drawing cards yourself, and receiving a reading inside a calm, immersive world.
